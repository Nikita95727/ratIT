## 📝 Content Guidelines

### Meta Descriptions

- Include call-to-action
- Include primary keyword
- Make it compelling

- Use H2 for section headings
- Use H3-H6 for subsections
- Include relevant keywords naturally

- Include relevant keywords

- Be descriptive but concise
